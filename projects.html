R packages

The R packages can be installed using the ``devtools`` package. For instance, ``devtools::install_github("nathanesau", "m4fe")``.

  • makehams Code for SOA Exam MLC with focus on survival models such as Makeham's Law.
  • MSM Code for SOA Exam MLC with focus on multiple state models.
  • m4fe Code for SOA Exam MFE. On CRAN.
  • lmodels Code for SOA Exam C.
  • StocVal Research project from Summer 2015. Risk-neutral valuation of embedded options in pension plans. Reproducing results from SOA research study.
  • stocins Stochastic interest rate moels for life insurance.

Case competitions

  • Asna2016Actuarial Students National Association 2016 competition related to analyzing the cost effectiveness of automobile safety features.
  • MunichReCup2016 Munich Re Cup 2016 Competition related to analyzing insurer strategies to deal with high prescription drug costs.
  • ukencompetition Simon Fraser University Statistics Department 2015 competition related to using data science methods to predict revenue earned from in-app purchases in a mobile app game.
